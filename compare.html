    Honest take

    Same shifting performance and gear range. SLX is 210 g lighter for €200 more — worth it if you race or ride hard 3+ times a week. For 1–2 rides weekly, Deore is all you need.

    Deore M6100

    • Excellent value for 12-speed performance
    • Wide gear range (510%)
    • Cross-compatible with SLX/XT chain and cassette
    • Heavier than higher tiers
    • Requires Micro Spline freehub (not HG)
    • Brake feel basic compared to MT520

    SLX M7100

    • Significantly lighter than Deore
    • Smoother shifting, better clutch
    • Cross-compatible with Deore and XT
    • €200 more than Deore for marginal gains for casual riders
    • XT is only €100–150 more — many will skip SLX
    • Same brake feel as Deore
