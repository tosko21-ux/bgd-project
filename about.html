About

bikegeardecoder.com is a project by a hobby cyclist for lazy hobby cyclists — and any other cycling enthusiast who rarely makes it past the first paragraph. The goal is to compare the components on your bike as plainly as possible, without forcing you through endless research and spec sheets. Hopefully it makes things easier when you're picking a new bike, or figuring out where to go next with upgrades to your trusty steed.

— Tomas, founder of BGD
